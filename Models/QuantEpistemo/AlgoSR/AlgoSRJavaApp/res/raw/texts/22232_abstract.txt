To analyse risk through space in Quito and in Lima, the author choose a geopolitical way comparing the differents social perceptions of risk, in especial the causes, between stakes and vulnerability reasons. The article show how the risk’s spaces are assimilated to deprived areas carrying stigma. The stigma’s analysis collaborating with geosciences is a way to understand social and political characteristics of risk and to identify risk as a territorialisation’s tool of deprived areas.
