The advanced state of the decrepitude of the old building of the Algerian city poses the dilemma of the demolition, no longer as tool-sanction but rather as “legitimated” tool toward the need for renewing it and for restructuring it. The recent changes of the Algerian city in general and of Annaba in particular require the introduction of new tools to accompany these transformations ; transformations resulting from private initiatives encasing the step with the public action.
