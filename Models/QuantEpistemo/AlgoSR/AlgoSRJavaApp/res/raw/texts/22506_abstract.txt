Paleoanthropology offers an ideal practising ground to grasp the relationship scientists entertain with the space when trying to build up the scenarios of the evolution. In these attempts, the geographical determinism, declined in ecosystematic, climatic, even geologic determinism, is often the rule. Revisiting the famous and much publicized theory of East Side Story shows how the narration takes over from the scientific argumentation and underlines the difficulty of “thinking in million year terms”. Such second reading is particularly important at a moment when the creationist thesis become stronger.
