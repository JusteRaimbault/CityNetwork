The emphasis of Geographic Information System (GIS) was on spatial data structures and general spatial data analysis. GIS has a large rang of applications and it is going to become a common tool in modern human life style, even now people wish more and more information from GIS. People wish representation of spatial data not only a static but a dynamic spatial data also, for this purpose a Temporal Geographic Information System (TGIS) was introduced in recent years.
